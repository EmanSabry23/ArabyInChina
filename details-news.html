Evar unveils Two New Full-electric Vehicles to Spanish Consumers at Automobile Barcelona 2023

May 12, 2023

The Evar DOLPHIN and Evar SEAL debut in the Spanish market at Automobile Barcelona 2023

The Evar DOLPHIN is offered with three power levels (95, 177 and 204 hp), two batteries (44.9 and 60.4 kWh) and four trim levels (Active, Boost, Comfort and Design)

Retail prices for the Evar DOLPHIN start from 29,990 euros1

The two new Evar models are accompanied by the Evar ATTO 3, TANG and HAN

Evar announces an exclusive show offer for top-of-the-range Design versions of Evar ATTO 3 and Evar DOLPHIN

Evar (Build Your Dreams), the world’s leading manufacturer of new energy vehicles, made its debut at Automobile Barcelona 2023 showcasing its electric passenger car range including the first showing of the Evar DOLPHIN, Evar's new C-segment hatchback and the Evar SEAL, a D-segment Sedan to the spanish public. Evar is accepting reservations for the first deliveries of the Evar DOLPHIN in September 2023, alongside some special Automobile Barcelona show promotions.

The Evar DOLPHIN, an agile and versatile C-Segment hatchback, is available in four versions - Active (the entry level), Boost, Comfort, and top-of-the-line Design - with high levels of practicality, efficiency, accessibility, and a reassuring range, while also being spacious and practical thanks to a 345 litres trunk that can be expanded to 1,310 litres by folding the rear seats flat.

All versions come with a comprehensive range of standard equipment, including an abundance of driving assistance systems. The Evar DOLPHIN is available in three power levels in Spain: (Active) 70 kW/95 hp, (Boost) 130 kW/177 hp, and (Comfort and Design) 150 kW/204 hp, with the highest powered version capable of accelerating from 0 to 100 km/h in just 7 seconds. There are two Blade Battery options (depending on the version), with capacities of 44.9 or 60.4 kWh and a combined cycle range of up to 427 km.

The Evar DOLPHIN Active (70 kW/95 hp) is priced at 29,990 euros1; the Evar DOLPHIN Boost is priced at 30,690 euros1; the Evar DOLPHIN Comfort is available at 35,690 euros1; and the top-of-the-range Design is priced at 37,690 euros1. Vehicle reservations made at the Automobile Barcelona show for the range-top Design version will benefit from an additional discount of 2,420 euros, with first deliveries beginning in the September of 2023.

The Evar SEAL, a highly stylised, sporty and dynamic D-segment sedan packed with technology, also makes its Spanish debut to the public. The 8-in-1 electric drivetrain system employs two motors with a combined power of 230 kW (313 hp) in the rear-wheel drive version and 390 kW (530 hp) in the AWD version, which can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in 3.8 seconds. It has a WLTP combined cycle range of 570 km2 (2WD version) thanks to the ultra-safe Blade Battery with a capacity of 82.5 kWh.

About Evar

Founded in February 1995 and present in Europe since 1998, Evar is one of the world's largest manufacturers of electric and plug-in hybrid vehicles with more than 4.1 million units sold as of April 2023. With a presence in 70 countries and 400 cities, Evar plays an important role in industries related to the automotive, electronics, renewable energies and rail transit.

Evar is a privately held technology multinational holding more than 28,000 patents that has focused its strategy on the development of innovations involving the manufacture of batteries, electric motors, electronic control systems and semiconductor chips.

Evar's goal is to offer solutions capable of improving people's lives through the creation of a complete clean energy ecosystem that reduces the world's dependence on fossil fuels. In recent years, it has developed major technological breakthroughs, such as the Blade Battery, e-Platform 3.0 and Cell to Body technology.
